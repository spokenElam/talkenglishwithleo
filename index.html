How the Service Works

1. Receive Daily Tasks

Every morning, your British tutor will send a personalized "micro-task" on WhatsApp or WeChat, based on your learning goals.

2. Complete the Task Anytime

You only need to complete the task during any free moment within 24 hours, such as recording a voice message or writing a response.

3. Get Personalized Feedback

Your tutor will analyze your response in detail, help you improve pronunciation, and guide you on how to express yourself more naturally. You are guaranteed to receive this dedicated feedback within 24 hours.

4. Weekly Synthesis Summary

Every weekend, you will have a 10-minute instant text chat with your tutor to summarize the week's learning progress.

Your Learning Journey

Our service is a professional learning journey. The tutor will first assess your level and start by building a solid foundation:

Phase One: Building Clear Expression

Focus on correcting grammar, basic pronunciation, and sentence structure. The primary goal is to help you speak naturally and accurately, making you easy to understand.

Phase Two: Pursuing Authentic Fluency (Optional)

Once your tutor confirms your foundation is solid, you can choose this advanced path. The tutor will teach British slang, authentic phrases, and provide voice feedback to fine-tune your British accent.
