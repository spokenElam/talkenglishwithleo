Our Weekly Study Plan

We use a structured weekly plan to systematically build your speaking skills from foundation to practical application.

Flexible Topics: We offer practical themes each week (e.g., "Ordering Food," "Asking for Directions"). You choose the topic that fits your interest or needs.

1 Monday: Vocab & Pronunciation

  • Master core vocabulary for the weekly theme
  • Use audio samples to perfect your pronunciation
  • Submit a voice recording for detailed feedback

2 Tuesday: Sentence Building

  • Build your own sentences using the new words
  • The coach reviews your audio submission
  • Corrections are provided for grammar and speaking mistakes

3 Wednesday: Practical Output - Asking

  • Practice asking questions in a simulated real-life scenario (e.g., asking for directions)
  • The coach responds via role-play
  • Receive feedback to optimize your natural phrasing

4 Thursday: Practical Q&A

  • Respond quickly to the coach's questions via recording
  • Feedback ensures your answers are accurate and complete
  • Focus on developing fluency in conversation

5 Friday: Weekly Sync-up

  • Progress review and summary
  • A scheduled text chat to summarize the week
  • The coach highlights your common errors and answers any questions you have

How the Service Works (The Daily Cycle)

  • Your experienced coach sends a personalized "micro-task" on WhatsApp or WeChat, tailored to the day's focus and your chosen theme
  • You complete the task during any free moment within 24 hours (voice recording or written response)
  • Your coach provides dedicated, detailed feedback on your submission, focusing on pronunciation and natural expression for maximum learning retention

In addition to our core daily coaching, we offer customized training for specific needs (e.g., interviews, business presentations, public speaking skills). Such services require a separate quotation.
