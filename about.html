About Us

Talk English with Leo

Our Mission

Talk English with Leo (operated by E-Lam Limited) was founded with a simple goal: to help learners like you do more than just study grammar rules—we want to help you speak fluent, authentic English with confidence.

We understand that many people study English for years in school but still hesitate when it comes to real-life conversation. That's why we designed our unique "Daily Focus" method, immersing you in a genuine language environment every day with personalized feedback from professional tutors.

Our Learning Model

We believe that consistent, structured practice is the key to success. Unlike traditional courses, we plan a systematic workflow from Monday to Friday, ensuring you master everything from input to output:

  • Mon - Tue: Focus on Vocabulary & Sentence Building to lay a solid foundation.
  • Wed - Thu: Enter Simulation Mode to practice asking questions and responding instantly.
  • Friday: Join the Weekly Sync-up to review progress and clarify doubts.

With flexible weekly themes (e.g., Ordering at a Restaurant, Asking for Directions), we ensure your learning is relevant to your life.

Our Tutors

We take pride in our team of Expat Tutors. Every tutor is an experienced expatriate who not only masters the English language but also deeply understands the cultural nuances behind it. They specialize in teaching authentic usage and are dedicated to helping you overcome the fear of speaking.

Our Promise

We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized, and effective English coaching. We believe that through daily practice and guidance from professional tutors, anyone can communicate in English with confidence.
