About Us

Welcome to TalkingBob, operated by E-Lam Limited. Our mission is simple: to help you speak English confidently, clearly, and fluently, with an authentic British accent.

We understand that many learners study English for years but still struggle to express themselves naturally in conversation. That's why we created our unique daily coaching program.

Our Approach

We believe in practical application and personalized feedback. Our "Daily Mission + Coach Feedback" model connects you directly with experienced tutors via WhatsApp or WeChat. Instead of rigid lessons, you get manageable daily tasks designed to build your skills progressively. The core of our service is the detailed, personalized feedback you receive within 24 hours, focusing on clarity, fluency, and pronunciation.

Our Tutors

All our coaching is provided by senior British tutors with over 10 years of residency in the UK. They possess a deep understanding of authentic British English, including nuances, idioms, and cultural context, ensuring you learn to speak like a local.

We are passionate about helping you achieve your English speaking goals. Join us and start speaking confidently, every day!
